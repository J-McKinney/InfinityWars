How To Become an Avenger...

Rules:

Thanos is waiting for you in the Nova Galaxy. Hurry and get there to confront him for the possession of the Infinity Stones.

There are 6 Infinity Stones below. By clicking on an Infinity Stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your Total Infinity Points to Thanos' Points, you lose the game if your Total Infinity Points goes above Thanos' Points.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the Infinity Stones values will too.

Good Luck and help the Avengers save the Universe...
